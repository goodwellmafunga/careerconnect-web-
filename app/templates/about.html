About CAREERCONNECT

CAREERCONNECT is a premier institution dedicated to providing top-notch education and career development opportunities. Located at Number 1 Nkwame, Harare, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and innovation in education.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through education and training, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their careers.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in providing innovative and quality education that transforms lives and communities.

Our History

Established in 1995, CAREERCONNECT has grown from a small community college to a comprehensive institution offering a wide range of courses and programs. Over the years, we have expanded our facilities and faculty to better serve our students and community.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, from teaching to student services.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovation and encourage creative thinking and problem-solving.
  • Community: We are committed to serving our community and making a positive impact.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of integrity and ethics in all our actions.
