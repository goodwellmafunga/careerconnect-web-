About CAREERCONNECT

CAREERCONNECT is a premier institution dedicated to providing top-notch education and career development opportunities. Located at Number 1 Nkwame, Harare, we are committed to empowering individuals through education and training. Our goal is to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their careers, fostering an environment of innovation and excellence. At CAREERCONNECT, we believe in the transformative power of education and strive to be a leading institution in providing innovative and quality education that transforms lives and communities.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through education and training, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their careers.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in providing innovative and quality education that transforms lives and communities.

Our Values

  • Respect: We respect and value diversity and inclusivity.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in education and training.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our actions.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovative methods to enhance learning and development.
  • Commitment: We are committed to the personal and professional growth of our students.
  • Community: We foster a strong sense of community and collaboration among our students and staff.
